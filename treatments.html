Treatments & Therapy Support

The right therapies can transform a child's life

Although Cerebral Palsy currently has no permanent cure, early intervention and continuous therapy can greatly improve a child's mobility, communication, independence and quality of life.

Our therapy programs

Care tailored to every child

Each child receives a plan shaped around their own needs, strengths and goals.

Physiotherapy

Improves muscle strength, mobility, flexibility and posture — helping children move with more confidence.

Occupational therapy

Helps children perform daily activities — dressing, eating, writing — more independently.

Speech therapy

Improves communication, language and swallowing abilities, and supports social interaction.

Behavioral therapy

Supports emotional regulation, social skills and positive behaviour development.

Special education

Customised learning support designed around each child's developmental needs.

Nutrition & medical guidance

Supports healthy growth, feeding and overall wellbeing alongside therapy.

A note of hope for parents

Progress in CP is rarely a straight line — but it is real. With consistent therapy, encouragement and a supportive environment, children continue to surprise us with what they can achieve.

Our role is to make these therapies accessible, to guide parents in continuing care at home, and to keep families motivated through every small victory.

Support for parents

Parent training programs

Therapy works best when it continues at home.

  • Guided home exercises
  • Safe handling & positioning tips
  • Daily-routine integration
  • Progress tracking made simple
