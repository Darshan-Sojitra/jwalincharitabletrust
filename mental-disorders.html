Mental & Developmental Disorders

Understanding developmental disorders in children

Many children experience developmental and mental health challenges that affect how they learn, communicate, move and interact. With understanding, early support and the right care, every child can grow and thrive.

Conditions we support

Common developmental & mental disorders

Developmental disorders

Delays in physical, cognitive, language or social development that benefit from early support.

Autism Spectrum Disorder

Affects communication, social interaction and behaviour, with each child experiencing it differently.

ADHD

Attention-Deficit / Hyperactivity Disorder affects focus, impulse control and activity levels.

Intellectual disability

Affects learning, reasoning and everyday skills, with support tailored to each child.

Speech & learning disorders

Challenges with speech, language, reading or learning that respond well to targeted therapy.

Emotional & behavioral disorders

Support for emotional regulation, behaviour and social-emotional development.
